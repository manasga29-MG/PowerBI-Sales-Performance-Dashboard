# PowerBI-Sales-Performance-Dashboard
Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard for Maven Fuzzy Factory to analyze sales performance, profitability, product trends, customer behavior, and return metrics. Implemented Power Query for data transformation, DAX measures for KPI calculations, and interactive visualizations to provide business insights for executive decision-making.
> An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product trends, and return metrics to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Maven Fuzzy Factory Sales Performance Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI**. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, evaluate product performance, and make informed strategic decisions.

## 🎯 Business Problem

The management team required a centralized dashboard to answer key business questions such as:

- How is the business performing month-over-month?
- Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
- Which regions contribute the most to sales?
- What is the return quantity trend?
- How are customers contributing to revenue?
- Which KPIs should executives monitor regularly?

Without a unified reporting solution, business decisions were based on fragmented reports and manual analysis.

---

- Track revenue, profit, and sales quantity.
